## FAQ

**Q: What counts as a scan?**  
A: One URL = one scan. Subpages require separate scans.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Yes, within 30 days for annual plans.

**Q: Do you store URLs I scan?**  
A: No, URLs are not stored. Only violation data is retained.
